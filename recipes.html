Brain-Boosting Omega Bowl

This nutrient-dense bowl combines wild-caught salmon, avocado, and leafy greens for optimal brain health.

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 wild-caught salmon fillets (6oz each)
  • 1 large avocado, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Sea salt and pepper to taste
